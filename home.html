SO KIDS CAN MOVE

INTRODUCTION

By encouraging state-by-state legislative action and increasing access to orthotic and prosthetic care for active lifestyles, So Kids Can Move aims to continue to challenge healthcare injustice with a policy solution founded on dignity and justice. 28x28 is our ultimate objective. To pass this legislation in 28 states before the 2028 Paralympics in Los Angeles, igniting a movement for change across the country and offering the chance for pursuing federal, nationwide reform.

WHY THIS IS A PROBLEM

Alex was living an active lifestyle, like swimming, biking, running, and hiking before he had stage II bone cancer on his knee when he was 15 years old. Alex got amputated to stop the spreading of cancer. With this video, he wants to shine a light to the cost of assistive technology that people with disabilities have to face.

NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS

Ability Found This organization helps patients find different kinds of medical equipment at low to no cost, but it does not offer assistance with prosthetics or orthotics. This covers a wide variety of things, such as shower chairs, hospital beds, manual and electric wheelchairs, gait trainers, grab bars, tub benches, and more. A medical expert must submit your application on your behalf. Please be aware that products that need to be sent or modified to meet your demands can call for a small amount of money from the person to cover shipping costs.
Amputee Blade Runners Amputee Blade Runners works to give amputees access to free running prosthetics. Since insurance companies do not pay for running prostheses because they are deemed "not medically necessary," this charity aids amputees in maintaining an active way of life.
Global Reach Bionics Global Reach Bionics uses modern innovations to create contemporary remedies for amputees in challenging circumstances when an effective prosthetic seems unreachable. We aims to give prosthetic limbs to those in need absolutely free of charge as a non-profit company. Moving forward, they support less fortunate amputees by creating free and inexpensive prosthetic equipment, improving people's quality of life.
Mending Limbs Organization If your insurance fails to cover the cost of your prosthetics, Mending Limbs is able to assist.
